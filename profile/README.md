# BetterZamPen

**Building a better Zamboanga Peninsula** - Making government information and services accessible to everyone in Region IX.

## Our Mission

BetterZamPen is a community-led initiative to create a modern, accessible, and user-friendly government information portal for the Zamboanga Peninsula. We're part of the [BetterGov Philippines](https://github.com/bettergovph) movement.

## What We're Building

- **Comprehensive LGU Directory** - Complete contact information for all cities and municipalities across the peninsula
- **Government Services Portal** - Easy access to public services
- **Transparency Data** - Budgets, projects, and procurement information
- **Emergency Resources** - Hotlines and facilities directory
- **Local Language Support** - Cebuano/Bisaya and Chavacano translations

## Coverage Area

**Region IX - Zamboanga Peninsula**

### Zamboanga del Norte
- **2 Cities**: Dipolog (Capital), Dapitan
- **27 Municipalities**

### Zamboanga del Sur
- **2 Cities**: Pagadian (Capital), Zamboanga City
- **26 Municipalities**

### Zamboanga Sibugay
- **1 City**: Ipil (Capital)
- **16 Municipalities**

### Basilan
- **1 City**: Isabela City
- **11 Municipalities**

**Total Coverage**: ~4 million residents served

## Development Phases

**Phase 1**: Zamboanga del Norte (starting with Dipolog City)  
**Phase 2**: Zamboanga del Sur  
**Phase 3**: Zamboanga Sibugay  
**Phase 4**: Basilan
